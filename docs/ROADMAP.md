# Music Platform Roadmap

## Phase 0: Deep Design (Seed/Pre-Launch) — Next 1-2 Months

**Goal:** Lock in product vision, behavioral frameworks, and monetization clarity.

**Deliverables:**
- [ ] Complete product spec (every feature, every decision justified)
- [ ] User journey mapping (acquisition → onboarding → engagement → retention)
- [ ] Behavioral psychology framework (which behaviors do we reward?)
- [ ] Monetization playbook (how do partnerships work?)
- [ ] Network outreach plan (which labels, managers, artists first?)
- [ ] MVP feature list (ruthlessly prioritize)

**Why this matters:** Avoid building features we'll regret. Avoid AI-influenced thinking anchoring our vision.

---

## Phase 1: MVP Launch (Months 3-5) — 0 → 10K DAU

**Goal:** Build a product so good that early users become evangelists.

### Product (Ruthlessly Simple)
- [ ] User profiles with taste summary
- [ ] Share a song/album with written thoughts
- [ ] Comments & discussion on shares (upvote best comments)
- [ ] Follow friends & curated accounts
- [ ] Feed algorithm (simple at first, intentional)
- [ ] Discover page (trending by genre/region/community)
- [ ] No bots, no AI suggestions, no Twitter garbage

### Growth
- [ ] Closed beta with 500 die-hard music fans (hand-recruited from your network)
- [ ] Ambassador program (musicians, producers, talent managers)
- [ ] Seeded with early community (Reddit, Discord, Twitter)
- [ ] Network effects instrumentation (track referrals, invites, shares)
- [ ] Analytics dashboard (obsess over retention curves)

### Monetization
- [ ] First label/manager partnership (1-2)
- [ ] Test exclusive drop: New music preview, artist AMA, or concert discount
- [ ] Measure engagement lift from partnerships

### Success Metrics
- **10K DAU** (could be lower, but this shows product-market fit)
- **30%+ W1 retention**
- **Referral coefficient > 1.2** (each user brings >1.2 friends)
- **30+ min average session duration**

## Phase 2: Growth & Network (Months 6-12) — 10K → 100K DAU

**Goal:** Prove the flywheel works. Scale distribution through partnerships.

### Product
- [ ] Genre-specific communities (subculture optimization)
- [ ] "Notable Commenter" badges (verified musicians, music critics, super fans)
- [ ] Share to Instagram/TikTok (let network effects spread beyond app)
- [ ] Playlist collaboration
- [ ] Artist pages (if they join, official status)
- [ ] Integration: Spotify/Apple Music (what to listen to, what others listen to)

### Growth
- [ ] 5-10 major label partnerships live
- [ ] Talent manager network activated (artists encouraging fans to join)
- [ ] University campus seeding (college-aged users)
- [ ] Influencer/ambassador program scaled
- [ ] Press & awards (music tech recognition)

### Monetization
- [ ] 3-5 partnership revenue streams live
  - Concert ticket partnerships (Live Nation, AXS, etc.)
  - Merchandise drops (artist-exclusive apparel)
  - Music discovery playlist partnerships
  - Premium artist features (artist profile boost, custom landing page)
  - Events (exclusive listening parties, meet-and-greets)

### Success Metrics
- **100K DAU**
- **25%+ W4 retention**
- **$X monthly recurring from partnerships** (TBD based on agreements)
- **Network effects clear** (invites driving >50% new user signups)

## Phase 3: Monetization & Scale (Months 13-24) — 100K → 1M+ DAU

**Goal:** Prove the business model. Build sustainable unit economics.

### Product
- [ ] Live events platform (in-app ticketing to exclusive concerts, listening parties)
- [ ] Prediction markets (optional betting on music trends, chart positions)
- [ ] Creator fund (revenue share for top contributors/musicians)
- [ ] Regional localization (language, local artists, local events)
- [ ] International expansion

### Growth
- [ ] International label partnerships
- [ ] Artist partnership at scale (100+ official artist profiles)
- [ ] Category definition (become synonymous with "music social")
- [ ] Cross-platform presence (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube shorts)

### Monetization
- [ ] Live events driving 20%+ of revenue
- [ ] Premium subscription (optional, for power users)
- [ ] Advertising (native, non-intrusive, from partners only)
- [ ] Creator fund operational
- [ ] Betting/prediction revenue (if live)

### Success Metrics
- **1M+ DAU**
- **$X MRR from partnerships + events + premium**
- **Unit economics positive** (LTV:CAC > 3)
- **Category ownership** (people say "it's like Letterboxd but for music")

## Critical Guardrails (All Phases)

### Product Integrity
- **No AI-generated content.** Every piece of data is human-sourced.
- **No bots or fake engagement.** Ban aggressively.
- **No algorithm toxicity.** Monitor for trolls, misinformation, hateful content.
- **Cybersecurity first.** Require actual human interaction (no API scrapers, no automation).

### Data Science First
- **Every decision is measured.** If we can't measure it, we debate it; if we can, we decide by data.
- **Cohort analysis.** Understand user segments deeply. What drives retention for 18-24 year olds vs. 35-45?
- **Behavioral economics.** Why do people share? Why do they stay? Design for those motivations.
- **Rapid iteration.** A/B test aggressively. Kill features that don't move the needle.

### Community Health
- **Moderation-first.** Small team, big impact. Remove toxicity immediately.
- **Wholesome culture.** Be the anti-Twitter. Reward thoughtful discussion, not hot takes.
- **Creator respect.** Never incentivize low-effort content. Reward depth.
